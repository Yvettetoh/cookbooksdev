## Tips

### PEMDAS Meaning

- P - Parentheses first
- E - Exponents
- MD - Multiplication and division (left to right)
- AS - Addition and subtraction (left to right)

### Operators

- Addition: `5 + 2 = 7`
- Subtraction: `5 - 2 = 3`
- Multiplication: `5 * 2 = 10`
- Division: `5 / 2 = 2.5`
- Exponent: `3 ** 2 = 9`
- Floor Division: `5 // 2 = 2`
- Modulus: `5 % 2 = 1`
