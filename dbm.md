# Digital Business Metrics (DBM)

## Metrics

- Change Failure Rate (CFR)
- Deployment Frequency (DF)
- Lead Time for Changes (LTC)
- Mean Time Between Failures (MTBF)
- Mean Time To Conflict Resolution (MTTCR)
- Mean Time to Repair (MTTR)
- Time to Market (TTM)
