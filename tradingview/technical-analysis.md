## Tendencies

The **trend** is your **friend**.

- **Primary:** Longer graphics times (e.g. Monthly)
- **Secondary:** Intermediate graphics times (e.g. Monthly)
- **Tertiary:** Lower graphics times (e.g. Monthly)

### RSI Uptrend Oversold
